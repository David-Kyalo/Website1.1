In the bleak mid winter, Asguard stood on the slab facing the chilly slopes of St. Perencia hills. Ofcourse the mist barred him from seeing the foot of the hill where a bustling Mulei Town had just picked pace and shops were opening. It was a beutiful sight to behold, especially one who is about to adopt a life of a fugitive.

Flashbacks of the previous night fresh in his mind. A subtle shake of the head showing his disappointment on how things went. "That was not how it was supposed to end", he said in monologue. Knowing the police will be at his door step soon enough, he picked up his black duffel bag and clenched on his pendant.
